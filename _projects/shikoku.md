In the spring of 2023, I embarked on an unforgettable adventure – the Shikoku Pilgrimage. For 45 days straight, alongside my companions, we traversed the island's 88 sacred temples, delving into the tranquility of its landscapes and the richness of Japanese culture.

Sure, there were moments of fatigue and muscle soreness along the way, but looking back, those challenges were just part of the journey. The pilgrimage wasn't just about visiting temples – it was about embracing the thrill of the unknown, creating memories, and discovering the resilience within ourselves.
