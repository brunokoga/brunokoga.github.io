I've had the privilege of working on the [Siilo app](https://www.siilo.com) since its inception in 2016, contributing to the iOS development since Siilo’s early days and helping shape it into Europe's go-to healthcare messenger for professionals. With a strong emphasis on privacy and security, Siilo boasts end-to-end encryption to ensure sensitive information remains protected at all times.
