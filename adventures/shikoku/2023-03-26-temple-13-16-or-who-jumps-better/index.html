Temple 13-16 (or who jumps better)

March 26, 2023

After an exhausting day yesterday going up the mountain ⛰️, we were rewarded today with a beautiful sight of the valley. Our host was also very kind to wash, dry and fold our clothes (and this lifts up the mood of any pilgrim 😊).

There was a long stretch before we could reach the first temple of the day, temple 13 (大日寺). Along the way we could once more enjoy the stunning views of the fully blossomed Sakura trees. After visiting temples 14 (常楽寺), 15 (国分寺) and 16 (観音寺) we reached our Ryokan, which is a fancier traditional japanese accommodation. Both me and Maike decided to put the vegetarianism aside and eat tuna sashimi 🍣 and we don’t regret: this Ryokan is known for its renowned chefs and the food was absolutely delicious 👨‍🍳😗 (chefs kiss).
