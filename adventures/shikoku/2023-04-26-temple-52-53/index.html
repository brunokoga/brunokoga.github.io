Temple 52-53

April 26, 2023

After having breakfast at a Starbucks 🫣 we walked around Matsuyama. We passed by the famous Dōgo Onsen (it’s a 3000+ years old bath house) but to be honest, we were not impressed. We enjoyed much more the colorful art exposition on the surroundings of the onsen.

From there we took a tram and a train to temple 52 and then walked to temple 53. The weather changed completely, and the day turned from a cold and rainy morning to a beautiful, sunny and warm afternoon.
