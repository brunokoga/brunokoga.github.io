Kuriositäten am Wegesrand

April 22, 2023

After walking for more than 700km we have seen quite some strange/funky/interesting things along the roads. Here are some of them:

1-3) Nature taking over - Random electronic waste in the middle of nowhere. We learned that giving up your car costs a lot of money here, so many people simply abandon their old cars in the forest.

  1. TAKENOKO! 🎍It’s a new growing bamboo tree (a baby bamboo 👶) You can eat the stem and it’s delicious!

  2. A teeny tiny toilet along the way. I think it’s for kids …

  3. Soft drinks vending machine paradise! 🧋🧃🥤

  4. A phone holder for the perfect picture 🤳

  5. A completely pink (!) gas station 💗💗💗

  6. „Do you want to build a snowman?“ ☃️Someone rebuild a winter scene and the Shikoku pilgrims in their classic Ohenro outfit.
