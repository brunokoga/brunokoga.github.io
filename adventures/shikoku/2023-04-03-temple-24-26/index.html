Temple 24-26

April 3, 2023

We finally visited temples again today (24, 25, 26)!

Aaaand that was about it for the day 😝 It was a quite uneventful, I dare to say almost ‘boring’ day, that we mostly spent with walking along busy roads. I felt like I was on autopilot mode most of the time 🤖 Walking, eating and more walking. Hiker’s Hunger is a real thing btw, it seems as if we are constantly eating just to keep on walking 🚶‍♀️

Speaking of food, tonight we had an amazing home-cooked dinner by our lovely host lady. We ate cooked bamboo sticks, deep fried weeds from the beach and squid, just to mention a few dishes. Our host was so cute and curious to talk to us and get to know us ✨ Thanks to Google translate we can now call each other friends! 😉 One more kind soul we met along our path 👣

That’s it for today, boa noite!

Maike 🧜‍♀️
