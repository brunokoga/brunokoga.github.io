What our days look like

April 19, 2023

Even though every day is unique and special on its own (especially when reflecting upon it in the evenings), the days all together feel more or less the same. They become this one big blur 🌀Sounds paradoxical but … do you know what I mean?

To demonstrate, I can explain to you our rhythm of a ‘normal’ day:

5am - 6am 🕕

The alarm rings at 5 but most of the time I don’t hear it and Bruno is already awake half an hour earlier anyways 🙃 We always always have breakfast otherwise we can’t leave the house 😜 Of course we eat good ol‘ Müsli, fruits and …

🙌peanutbutter🙌. After stretching we start our hike at 6.

10am 🕙

Time for a second breakfast! Best kind of breakfast hehe 🫠 If we’re lucky we find a convenience store around that time. There you can find all kinds of food and we buy some 🍙 and 🥕 and eat it in the parking lot (see picture). That’s the life! 😎

Until 3/4pm 🕓

Walking, walking, talking, eating, singing, walking, arriving! After the check-in at each place we are called via the room telephone to our showers. After shower we chill for a bit in a very hot tub called ofuro ♨️ It’s good for the sore muscles (and stretching again helps a lot too) 💪

6pm 🕕

Dinner time! We either buy some Bento 🍱 boxes at the supermarket, eat at a restaurant or if we have a kitchen we cook 👩‍🍳 Most of the times we’re lazy and … cook 😝

9pm 🕘

Hop in the kimono, quickly write a loooong Polarsteps post and then it’s time to sleep 😴 9pm is the new midnight here! With that said: Gute Nacht 😘

Maike 🐰
