Temple 54-59

April 27, 2023

After having used bikes, bus and train we can now proudly say that we have finally added a taxi 🚕 to our portfolio 🙌 Well, how did that happen? Keep reading.

This morning we took two trains and eventually started to walk at 8:30. Our latest start so far! At our second temple we had a nice conversation with a fellow pilgrim from Denmark 🇩🇰 which already put a smile on our faces 😊 Then, we ran into our friend from temple 27 (see post there): the pilgrim collector! Again, we wrote a little text in his book and again he took a picture with his flip phone. “Man sieht sich immer zweimal” like ze Germans say seems to be true!

Somehow though our day ended up being longer than expected, with the two pleasant surprises as mentioned above and a 20km day that turned out to be more of a 30km day 😮‍💨 When we got off our last train a beautiful, magical, very tempting taxi was waiting around … and suddenly arriving in 8 minutes by 🚕instead of 1 hour by 🚶‍♀️ becomes very tempting when you’re exhausted and hungry, let me tell you this! And the rest is history 🏛️
