First day without a temple

April 1, 2023

Since the distance from temple 23 to temple 24 is 77km, today was our first day with no temple visits. We chose also to take a slightly longer route to walk closer to the coast line and avoid the busy road (walking through tunnels is an experience that I don’t wish to anyone 😖). The path was full of beautiful views from the sea and from small beaches that we passed by along the way.

We stopped for lunch at an awesome burger place recommended by Maya (the japanese girl we met three days ago, on our way to temple 23). We had vegan burgers and strawberry muffins and they were delicious 🍔🧁. The owner (which is Maya’s friend) was very welcoming as well.

After arriving at our accommodation, we changed to our swimwear and went for a dip in the ocean at the beach (which is 5 minutes walk from where we’re staying)! We cooked a big big pan of vegetables for dinner and are now making plans for a tough day tomorrow: the first four or five accommodations that we tried to book for tomorrow were already full, so we’re staying at a hotel that’s 40km away 🥵.

Bruno 🏊
