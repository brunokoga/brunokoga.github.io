Planning how to escape the fire

July 20, 2024

Day 94

Mile 1465.0 to mile 1497.5

PCT Miles: 32.5 (52km)

Total Miles: 1497.5 (2396.0km)

Today was a good hiking day. Since leaving camp at 5:45 I kept a good pace. The trail was mostly flat and it’s much more enjoyable to hike early in the morning, before the sun shows up with full power.

My first real break was around noon, after having crushed 17 miles already. I took a 45 minute lunch break and then kept going for one and a half hour more. Time for another break (with trail angel candies) and to camel up 🐫🆙 for the next stretch: 10 miles uphill in the hottest time of the day.

At least here the trees 🌲 still have their leaves and they provided good shade. I kept more or less the same pace until I arrived at camp around 7:30pm after hiking 52km today!
