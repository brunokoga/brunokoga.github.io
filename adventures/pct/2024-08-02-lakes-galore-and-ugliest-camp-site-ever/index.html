Lakes galore and ugliest camp site ever

August 2, 2024

Day 107

Mile 1998.8 to mile 2031.9

PCT Miles: 33.1 (53.0km)

Total Miles: 2031.9 (3251.0km)

I still can’t believe on the low amount of mosquitoes that I’ve faced here in Oregon so far. I still have a few days around here so they might be preparing a sneak attack. But let’s keep our hopes high that they just won’t show up!

The hiking in the morning was very pleasant, with views of at least five beautiful lakes. I can’t say the same about the afternoon, where the astonishing landscape was replaced by miles and miles of burnt trees.

Not much else to share. I crossed paths with a few southbounders today, some thru hikers, some day hikers. Weather went up again so even though I’m staying in a horrible camp site tonight, I hope I can sleep better than the past chilly nights.
