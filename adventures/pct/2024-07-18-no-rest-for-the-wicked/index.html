No rest for the wicked

July 18, 2024

Day 92

Burney Mountain Guest Ranch to mile 1435.7

PCT Miles: 24.9 (39.8km)

Total Miles: 1435.7 (2297.1km)

What a lovely place! The Burney Mountain Guest Ranch was my favorite place to stay during the PCT so far!

We had waffles and potatoes and eggs and hash browns for breakfast and after some resistance from my body (who really wanted to stay and take a zero 0️⃣) I left BMGR a bit before 10am.

I then hiked 15 miles in exactly 5 hours (a super good pace!) and took a nice lunch break by a river, under a bridge (shade!). I initially had planned to hike only 15 miles today, but since I was feeling good, I kept hiking for five miles more until I found an awesome camping spot!
