Cold and windy

June 18, 2024

Day 62

Mile 1035.4 to mile 1054.4

PCT Miles: 19.0 (30.4km)

Total Miles: 1054.4 (1687.0km)

It was a cold night and a cold morning. It took me a while to gather the strength to leave the warmth of the sleeping bag and start packing. I left camp at 7am.

Around 10am I met Bullfrog on trail. He had an early start so he was ahead of me. He was having breakfast so I moved on.

Even though it was sunny, there was a chilly wind blowing during the whole day. The trail was quite pleasant, with not many snow patches or sketchy parts. I took my time to hike and camped early, at 5pm. Around 6pm Bullfrog showed up and set his tent here as well! As I write this post, he’s building a fire! Temperature will drop below freezing tonight. I’m going to sleep wearing all my clothes!
