May 11, 2024

Day 24

Buckhorn Campground (mile 392.2) to mile 418.4

PCT Miles: 26.2 (41.9km)

Total Miles: 418.4 (669.4km)

When you hike long distances there are always days that go by and nothing extraordinary happen, specially when you get used to the logistics of hiking. Days like today: the sun was shining, it started with an ascent and later a decent. I had to make sure to collect water at the right sources and manage my consumption, I ate five cliffs bar during the day and had watermelon electronola for lunch. I had an encounter with a snake. Late afternoon there was a glimpse of a storm but it was soon gone and the sun was back. At the end of the day I hiked a marathon.

Just a normal hiking day 😊!
