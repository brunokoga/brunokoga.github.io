Nearo into Snoqualmie Pass

August 15, 2024

Day 120

Mile 2390.4 to Snoqualmie Pass

PCT Miles: 5.4 (8.6km)

Total Miles: 2395.8 (3833.3km)

It was a double chill morning: it was really cold and the only time pressure I had was that the earlier I hiked the remaining 5.4 miles to “town”, the earlier I would have warm breakfast and hot coffee!

Arrived at the coffee shop around 9am and stayed until noon with Jesse, Lily and Ranger (we all camped together last night). I got my resupply box at the gas station and sorted my food for the next days.

From there I went to my accommodation: a chalet/lodge called Washington Alpine Club. They offer a good deal for hikers which includes dinner and breakfast, clean towels and loaner clothes (clothes to use while yours are being washed). Every guest has also to pick up a chore. Mine was to take the trash out.

I had the immense surprise of meeting Geezer one more time here at the WAC! I hadn’t seen him since I took the wrong trail in the Sierras, on day 50! We had pizza for lunch together and chatted about the past days! I really miss hiking with Geezer, Bullfrog and Tangent! The group that we kindly named the Harry Pottheads 🪄!

For dinner we had infinite spaghetti 🍝 and various salads 🥗. Some of the vegetables were

even grown at the property itself!

For the coming days, I’m going to slow down a little bit and I decided to bring a book. I found two books here that I could read and decided to bring The Adventures of Tom Sawyer with me. It’s the first time on the PCT that I’m carrying a book 📕!
