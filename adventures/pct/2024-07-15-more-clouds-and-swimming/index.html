More clouds and swimming

July 15, 2024

Day 89

Mile 1347.4 to mile 1370.8

PCT Miles: 23.4 (37.4km)

Total Miles: 1370.8 (2193.3km)

From waking up to hiking in 20 minutes!

Still a lot of dead trees. It was cloudy for almost the whole day. The path was flat and mega easy to hike.

With the overcast, the breaks to filter water also become quicker since I don’t need to drink so much. I listened to music or podcasts all day long.

I’m camped in this best camping spot since a while already. There’s a creek and there’s a great spot to swim (did already ✅). I arrived at camp at 4pm and had plenty of time to stretch, swim, cook, wash and dry my shirt, filter water and just chill.

Tomorrow the post office opens at 11am and the General Store at 9am. I’m 4 miles away from Old Station so I should be there by 8am. The plan is to get my box, recharge my devices a bit, hopefully have internet to get more info about the wildfires ahead and move on!
