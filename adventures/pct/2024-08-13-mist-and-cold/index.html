Mist and cold 🌫️

August 13, 2024

Day 118

Mile 2336.9 to mile 2366.8

PCT Miles: 29.9 (47.9km)

Total Miles: 2366.8 (3786.9km)

I’m glad that I brought warmer clothes for Washington. It was a chilly night and morning.

For most of the day I hiked through a very dense fog/mist. When hiking in the forest, this mist made everything wet and created a kind of rain, creating water drops that would fall from the tree leaves. When hiking on more exposed areas like ridges, I could see the thick mist moving, like a big cloud.

Besides that, not much to add: it was a nice and calm hiking day. I met some enthusiastic local hikers that congratulated me for being “almost there”, enjoyed a few sun breaks and finished the audiobook “Greenlights” for the second time!
