Last night on the PCT

August 22, 2024

Day 127

Ride from Mazama to Harts Pass, then Hartss Pass to mile 2638.3

PCT Miles: 13.6 (21.8km)

Total Miles: 2638.3 (4221.3km)

From Lions Den theres a trail angel who does shuttles to Harts Pass, about one our ride. There was a full van leaving at 6:30am and I was scheduled to leave with two other hikers at 9:30am.

With time to spare in the morning, I used one of the loaner bikes that they have here and went to the bakery. I had the best cinnamon roll that I ever had in my life, hands down. Good start for the penultimate day on the PCT!

At 9:30am, the two other hikers decided to take a zero (they were vortexed by the Lions Den and the cinnamon rolls) and I was the only hiker in the shuttle. I could lay down and I literally took a half hour nap!

On the trail itself nothing special. Its pretty cold, but hopefully it wont rain and Ill stay dry for my last day!

While I was having dinner, two southbounders arrived and joined me at camp: it was Pete and Nick! They finished the PCT earlier today and now theyre hiking back, since they dont want hike into Canada.
