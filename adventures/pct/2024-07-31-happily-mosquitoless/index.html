Happily mosquitoless

July 31, 2024

Day 105

Mile 1948.2 to mile 1980.2

PCT Miles: 32.0 (51.2km)

Total Miles: 1980.2 (3168.3km)

For the past two nights I’ve been setting my tent up with the rain fly, so it serves as a barrier against the chilly wind. Therefore I was accidentally prepared for the rain that fell this past night.

In the morning the rain had stopped but I left camp wearing my rain pants and jacket, not against the rain but against the mosquitoes. To be fair there were very few in the morning, but I was sure it was going to get worse during the day. It didn’t.

Hiking was chill, with good weather and improving smoke situation. The water sources today were much better than the past days and surprisingly still very few mosquitoes!

Tomorrow I will get my second and last package here in Oregon. I’m not sure what to do: I’m getting really sick of my Oregon cold food. I can’t wait to have my stove back!
