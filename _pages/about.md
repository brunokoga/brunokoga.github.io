Hi 👋, I’m Bruno! Born and raised in Brazil, currently living a [nomadic lifestyle](/projects/nomad). 

After dedicating nearly 15 years to crafting iOS apps professionally, I decided to shake things up and transitioned into the role of Engineering Manager at [Doctolib Siilo](https://www.siilo.com). 

A few years back, I discovered a passion for sports. I'm particularly drawn to active sports in nature, whether it's shredding down snowy slopes on my snowboard or hiking along iconic trails. Hiking, in particular, has got me addicted, and I've set my sights on [conquering the Pacific Crest Trail in 2024](/projects/pct).
If you're as enthusiastic about outdoor adventures as I am, feel free to check out my [Strava profile](https://www.strava.com/athletes/brunokoga) and join me on my journey!

If you want to know more about me you can check out my [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bruno-koga/) and [Github](https://github.com/brunokoga) pages. I also highlighted some of my favorite projects [here](/projects).
